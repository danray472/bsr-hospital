Specialized Services

Neurosurgery

Our Neurosurgery services provide cutting-edge surgical treatments for complex brain, spine, and nervous system conditions. Using advanced technology and minimally invasive techniques, our expert neurosurgeons ensure precise, effective care for tumors, trauma, stroke, and neurological disorders, promoting faster recovery and improved outcomes.

Spine Surgery

Our Spine Surgery services offer advanced surgical solutions for spinal disorders, injuries, and degenerative conditions. Using minimally invasive techniques and state-of-the-art technology, our specialists provide precise treatment for herniated discs, spinal stenosis, fractures, and deformities, ensuring pain relief, mobility restoration, and faster recovery.

General Surgery

Our General Surgery services provide a wide range of surgical procedures for conditions affecting the abdomen, skin, soft tissues, and more. From minimally invasive techniques to complex operations, our expert surgeons ensure precise treatment, faster recovery, and optimal patient outcomes.

Stroke Management

Our Stroke Management program offers rapid emergency care, advanced treatment, and personalized rehabilitation to improve recovery outcomes. From clot-busting therapies to post-stroke rehabilitation, our multidisciplinary team ensures timely intervention, mobility restoration, and long-term support for stroke patients.

Orthopedic Surgery

Our Orthopedic Surgery services specialize in diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal conditions, including fractures, joint replacements, sports injuries, and spine disorders. Using advanced surgical techniques and minimally invasive procedures, we restore mobility, reduce pain, and enhance quality of life for our patients.

Outpatient Services

Neurosugery Consultation

Our Neurosurgery Consultations provide expert evaluation, diagnosis, and treatment planning for brain, spine, and nervous system conditions. Led by top neurosurgeons, we offer advanced imaging, minimally invasive solutions, and personalized care to ensure the best possible outcomes for each patient.

General Consultation

Our General Consultation services provide expert medical assessments for routine check-ups, symptom diagnosis, and chronic disease management. With a focus on preventive care, lifestyle guidance, and specialist referrals, we ensure personalized treatment plans for optimal health and well-being.

Outpatient Clinics

Our Outpatient Clinics offer specialized care in neurosurgery, orthopedics, pediatrics, and stroke management, providing expert consultations, diagnostics, and treatment plans without the need for hospitalization. With a focus on early intervention and personalized care, we ensure efficient, high-quality medical services for every patient.

Laboratory

Our Laboratory services provide a full range of medical tests and diagnostics, including blood work, imaging, pathology, and microbiology. Using advanced technology and precise analysis, we ensure fast, accurate results to aid in early detection, disease monitoring, and effective treatment planning

Physiotherapy

Our Physiotherapy focuses on rehabilitation, pain management, and restoring mobility for patients recovering from injuries, surgeries, or neurological conditions. Using personalized therapy plans, advanced techniques, and guided exercises, we help improve strength, flexibility, and overall quality of life.

Pharmacy

Our Pharmacy provides safe and accurate dispensing of prescribed medications, along with expert health consultations. We ensure proper medication management, patient education, and guidance on dosage, side effects, and drug interactions to support optimal treatment outcomes

Inpatient Services

Semi-private & Self-contained Wards

Our Semi-Private & Self-Contained Wards offer comfortable inpatient accommodations equipped with modern amenities, ensuring privacy, convenience, and quality care. With personalized medical attention, spacious rooms, and a healing environment, we prioritize patient comfort and recovery

ICU/HDU

Our ICU/HDU is a specialized unit for intensive care patients, offering specialized care for critically ill patients. The ICU/HDU provides specialized intensive and high-dependency care for critically ill patients, offering 24/7 monitoring, advanced life support, and expert medical intervention. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and a dedicated team, we ensure the highest level of care for patients in critical condition.

Neurorehabilitation

Our Neurorehabilitation services are designed for patients recovering from neurological conditions. Our Neurorehabilitation program offers specialized therapy for patients recovering from neurological conditions such as stroke, brain injuries, and spinal cord disorders. Using advanced techniques, personalized therapy, and assistive technology, we focus on restoring mobility, cognitive function, and independence for a better quality of life.
