About Us

Committed to excellence in healthcare

Our Story

BSR Hospital is a provider of acute and specialized Neurosurgery care and rehabilitation services.

BSR Hospital is located in a serene environment of Kamakis, Ruiru, Eastern bypass in Kiambu County. The facility is easily accessible both by public and private transport as it’s along the key artery of Thika Superhighway through Eastern Bypass.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional healthcare with compassion, integrity, and innovation.

Our Vision

To be the healthcare provider of choice, setting new benchmarks in patient care and medical excellence.

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, compassion, and excellence are at the heart of everything we do.

Our Core Values

COMPASSION

We treat everyone with courtesy, compassion, respect and dignity.

OPENNESS

We act with openness, honesty, and integrity.

PRIDE

We take pride in what we do and meeting our patient’s expectations.

PARTNERSHIP

IMPROVE

LEARN

We focus on mentorship, education, research and learning as our core pillars for growth.
