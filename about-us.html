About Us

The Brain Spine and Rehabilitation Hospital (BSR) is a surgical hospital offering specialized services involving disorders of the brain, spinal cord, nerves, orthopedics, trauma and general surgery. The brain surgeries include brain tumors, stroke and aneurysms, epilepsy and hydrocephalus among others. Spine surgery include spinal injuries, spinal tumors, spine degenerative diseases and spinal vascular malformations among others. Orthopedic and general surgeries are also part of our services. In summary, we provide outpatient services, inpatient, critical care, accident and emergency services, laboratory services, pharmacy, homecare and are in the process of establishing radiology services.

The hospital is a 40 bedded facility with a 7 bed Intensive Care Unit (ICU) and High Dependency Unit (HDU). We are equipped with a modern infrastructure including the state-of-the-Art C-Arm, neuro – endoscopy equipment and fully equipped operating theatres. We also have an Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) ambulance with CCTV monitoring capability and are in the process of installing a CT scanner so as to be a comprehensive stroke center.

Our Neuro rehabilitation unit is well equipped with modern technology aimed at re-establishing mobility in patients with spinal injuries, stroke and other conditions causing neurological weakness.
