Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer telemedicine services?

Yes, we provide telemedicine consultations. Please select the telemedicine option when booking your appointment.

What are your working hours?

We are open from 24/7hours Everyday

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule your appointment by calling our support team at least 24 hours in advance.

Do you provide rehabilitation and physiotherapy services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive physiotherapy and rehabilitation services to aid in recovery after neurosurgical procedures and other conditions.

How do I prepare for neurosurgery?

Before surgery, you may need imaging tests, blood work, and a consultation with our neurosurgical team. We will provide detailed preoperative instructions.

How long is the recovery time after spine surgery?

Recovery varies based on the procedure but typically ranges from a few weeks to several months. Our team will guide you through post-surgery rehabilitation.

Do you accept insurance for neurosurgical procedures?

Yes, we accept various insurance providers. Please contact our billing department for more details.
