About Us

The Brain Spine and Rehabilitation Hospital (BSR) is a specialized surgical hospital offering advanced care for brain, spine, orthopedic, trauma, and general surgery. We provide outpatient, inpatient, critical care, accident & emergency, laboratory, pharmacy, and homecare services. Our 40-bed facility includes a 7-bed ICU/HDU, modern surgical equipment, and an ACLS ambulance with CCTV monitoring. We are expanding our radiology services, including a CT scanner, to enhance stroke management. Our neuro-rehabilitation unit offers expert care for spinal injuries, stroke, and neurological conditions, with home-based therapy services.

Located in a serene environment of Kamakis, Ruiru, Eastern Bypass in Kiambu County, the facility is easily accessible by public and private transport along the key artery of Thika Superhighway.
